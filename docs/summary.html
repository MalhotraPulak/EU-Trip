Trip Highlights

Nature (4 days)

  1. Bohemian Switzerland - Europe's largest sandstone arch
  2. Vltava River Rafting - Float past Český Krumlov castle
  3. Austrian Lakes - Alpine beauty
  4. Budapest Thermal Baths - 500-year-old bathing culture

History & Culture (5 days)

  1. Prague Castle - World's largest ancient castle
  2. Český Krumlov - Fairytale medieval town
  3. Vienna - Imperial palaces, Klimt's "The Kiss"
  4. Budapest - Ottoman ruins, Jewish quarter
  5. Dresden - Baroque masterpiece (optional)

Party Nights (4-5 nights)

  1. Prague - Karlovy Lázně (Europe's largest club), Cross Club
  2. Vienna - Pratersauna, Flex
  3. Budapest - Ruin bars! Szimpla Kert, Instant-Fogas
  4. Budapest - SPARTY pool party (Saturday night!)
