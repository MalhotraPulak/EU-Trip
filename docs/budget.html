1. FLIGHTS (₹45,000 - ₹65,000)

India to Prague (Return)

Booking TimeOne-WayRound Trip

3 months advance₹25,000-35,000₹50,000-65,000
Last minute₹35,000-50,000₹70,000+

Best Airlines for Budget:

  • Turkish Airlines (via Istanbul) - Good balance of price/comfort
  • Azerbaijan Airlines (via Baku) - Often cheapest
  • LOT Polish Airlines (via Warsaw) - Good option
  • Qatar/Emirates - Premium but more expensive

Pro Tips:

  • Book 4-6 months in advance for April travel
  • April is shoulder season = better prices than summer
  • Use Google Flights, Skyscanner for price alerts
  • Consider flying into a different city if significantly cheaper

Budget Estimate: ₹50,000 (booking 4+ months early)

2. VISA & TRAVEL INSURANCE (₹12,000 - ₹15,000)

Schengen Visa:

  • Visa Fee: €80 (~₹7,200)
  • VFS Service Fee: ~₹2,500
  • Total Visa Cost: ~₹9,700

Travel Insurance (Mandatory for Schengen):

  • 15-day coverage: ₹1,500 - ₹3,000
  • Must have minimum €30,000 coverage
  • Get comprehensive policy with COVID coverage

Document Requirements:

  • Bank balance: ₹2+ lakhs recommended
  • Cover letter with detailed itinerary
  • Hotel bookings (refundable ones work)
  • Flight bookings (book refundable or after visa)

Budget Estimate: ₹13,000

Money Tips:

  • Get a forex card (Niyo, BookMyForex, Thomas Cook)
  • Load EUR primarily (most widely accepted)
  • Withdraw local currency from ATMs in Czech/Hungary
  • Inform your bank about travel dates
  • Always pay in local currency (avoid dynamic conversion)
  • Keep some EUR cash for emergencies

Cost-Saving Strategies

Biggest Savings:

  1. Book flights early - Save ₹15,000-20,000
  2. Airbnb with kitchen - Save ₹5,000-8,000 on food
  3. Free walking tours - Great for orientation
  4. Eat where locals eat - 50% cheaper than tourist areas
  5. Supermarket breakfasts - ₹200 vs ₹600 at cafes
  6. Budapest over Vienna - Everything is cheaper
  7. Travel off-peak - April is perfect timing

Don't Skimp On:

  1. Travel insurance - Mandatory and important
  2. Car insurance - Full coverage for peace of mind
  3. One nice dinner - You're on vacation!
  4. Thermal baths - Unique experience worth the money
  5. Ruin bars budget - The highlight of Budapest!
