## Safety Tips

### General
1. Stay together as a group
2. Designate a sober(ish) person
3. Share live locations on WhatsApp
4. Agree on meeting points if separated
5. Keep phones charged

### Drink Safety
1. Watch your drinks being poured
2. Don't accept drinks from strangers
3. If something tastes weird, don't finish it
4. Trust your instincts

### Scam Awareness
- "Pretty girl wants to buy you a drink" ‚Üí usually a scam
- Avoid strip clubs on Wenceslas Square (Prague)
- Check prices before ordering anywhere
- Get prices in writing at unfamiliar places

### Getting Home
1. Always have offline maps downloaded
2. Uber/Bolt work in all cities
3. Keep ‚Ç¨20-30 cash for emergencies
4. Know your accommodation address
5. Save it in Google Maps

### The Day After
- Hydrate, hydrate, hydrate
- Electrolytes help (Gatorade/ORS)
- Light breakfast
- Easy activities planned
- Thermal baths in Budapest = best hangover cure!
