Iter2 Red Flags

Critical issues with Iter2 that need to be addressed before it can work for your April 11-25 dates:

Day 1 Exhaustion Risk

High

Iter2 assumes you land in Vienna and immediately drive 245km to Budapest the same day. After an overnight flight from India, this is brutal and unsafe.

Duration Mismatch

Medium

Iter2 is designed for 16 days (Apr 10-25), but you only have 15 days on ground (Apr 11-25). You need to cut 1 night from somewhere.

No Buffer Days

Medium

Zero flexibility in the schedule. If weather is bad for Hallstatt/Nordkette, someone gets sick, or you want to extend a city you love - there's no slack.

Budapest to Prague (525km)

Medium

This is a 5.5-hour drive after only 2.5 days in Budapest. Feels rushed and tiring.

Heavy Driving Schedule

Low

7 significant driving days out of 15. Average 2.5-3 hours daily driving compared to Current's 1.5-2 hours.
